About songLab

What is songLab?

SongLab is a music collaboration website where users can browse and upload musical projects in need of creative collaboration.

Dreamt up by two cross-country friends who wanted to create music together, songLab allows

Are you a great lyricist but can't write music? A composer that can't do words? Do you have a song that needs percussion, or a recording that needs mixing? Upload your project to SongLab and find artists to help bring your song to life.
